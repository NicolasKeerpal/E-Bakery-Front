Our History

We have noticed that some people do not have access to bakeries near their homes, so they cannot enjoy what bakeries have to offer.
Furthermore, some people of foreign origins do not find pastries from their home countries in France, and therefore cannot find the foods they miss.

Therefore, we have created an online bakery named Lakojary. Lakojary is a 100% online bakery that offers pastries from all cultures through its online shop.
Thus, people who do not have access to bakeries, those who want to discover specialties from other cultures, or those who want to rediscover tastes from their own culture can do so with Lakojary!
